Description

Join an expert guide and explore the Sagrada Familia, Gaudi's masterpiece and Barcelona's most iconic landmark. On this 1.5-hour guided tour, you'll learn about the history of the basilica and the man who spent 40 years of his life building it.

See the Nativity and Passion facades, and go inside the church to see the stunning stained-glass windows and columns that look like trees. Your guide will explain the symbolism behind the architecture and why the project remains unfinished over 140 years later.
